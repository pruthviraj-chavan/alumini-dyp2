About DYP Salokhenagar Alumni Portal

The DYP Salokhenagar Alumni Portal is a dedicated platform designed to foster connections between current students and alumni of our prestigious institution. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where knowledge sharing, networking, and professional growth are encouraged.

Our Mission

To build a responsive, secure, and fast-loading web portal that enables verified communication between current students and alumni of DYP Salokhenagar. The portal allows text-only interactions, achievement/job postings, and private messaging, moderated and managed through an admin dashboard.

Key Features

  • Secure user authentication and verification system
  • Role-based access for students, alumni, and administrators
  • Text-only posts for achievements and job opportunities
  • Private messaging between students and alumni
  • Searchable directory of verified alumni and students
  • Admin moderation of all content and user verification

Get Involved

Whether you're a current student looking to connect with alumni for career guidance, or an alumnus wanting to give back to your alma mater by sharing opportunities and mentoring students, this portal is designed for you. Join our community today!
